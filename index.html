About me

Outsystems trainee developer
Frontend developer enthusiast
Former registered dietitian

Hello 👋🏽 Welcome to my page. I'm Inês and I'm 25 and I'm from Porto, Portugal.
I always though that I would be a dietitian and that I would love my job. (spoiler alert: that didn't happen!)
After graduating in 2017 I spent a year working in a nursing home. I liked it, but I couldn't imagine myself doing that for the rest of my life. So, I worked as an hostess for 2 years in different companies. Then, the COVID-19 hit the planet.
Without a job, I started my search to find a new path for my life.
One day, I found this Instagram ad about a coding workshop just for women. I decided to join and I spent the next 4 months completing homework and challenges.
That piqued my interest in the Tech World and made me consider it a possibility to my future.
Later I was accepted to a program that trains people in different IT areas.
I packed my bags, said goodbye to my city, and moved to Viseu to start this new journey.
I'm still a newbie, but let's see what the future holds for me!

I'm a vegetarian under construction 🌱 I like to travel 🌍 I want to live where summer lasts forever 😎 I love to cook (and eat) 🍜 I'm an Apple fan 📱 I like to meditate 🧘🏽‍♀️ I like to binge-watch series 🍿 I'm an adventurer 🎒
